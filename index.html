about us

The Heart and Mission of our Initiative

Founded on the principles of empathy, care, and social responsibility, our mission extends beyond mere assistance – we strive to instill hope and dignity in every life we touch.

From nurturing the youngest in our society with essential hygiene kits to providing warmth and comfort to the elderly through winter essentials, our actions are guided by a deep understanding of the needs of the vulnerable. Led by Pranati Mishra’s decades of expertise in child development and welfare, we're not just a trust, but a beacon of hope in our community.

Join us in our journey to spread smiles, one act of kindness at a time. Your support, whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word, plays a crucial role in sustaining our efforts. Together, let's make a difference that resonates for generations to come.

Spreading Hope, Uplifting Lives: Chronicles of Our Donation Campaigns

Donation
January 01, 2019
Chatighara Drive (First Round):

In this inaugural drive at Chatighara, our team successfully reached out to the community, providing essential items to 20 children and 98 adults, focusing on their immediate needs and enhancing their daily living conditions.

Donation
April 03, 2019
Sorisiapada Drive (First Round):

Our Sorisiapada drive marked a significant effort, where 54 children and 132 adults received necessary donations, deeply impacting the local community with our targeted assistance.

Donation
May 5, 2019
Fani Flood Relief Initiative

In response to the Fani flood disaster, our trust mobilized resources quickly to offer relief to 150 affected individuals, providing them with emergency essentials and support during this critical time.

Donation
February 25, 2020
Chatighara Drive (Second Round)

Returning to Chatighara for a second drive underscored our commitment to sustained support. This time, we expanded our reach, aiding 60 children and 97 adults, further strengthening our bond with the community.

Donation
April 13, 2022
Sorisiapada Drive (Second Round):

Our second visit to Sorisiapada was focused on consolidating our initial efforts, assisting an additional 35 children and 20 adults, ensuring ongoing support and care for those in need.

Donation
December 18, 2023
Khuntuni Drive

The Khuntuni drive was a robust initiative where we provided essential donations to 85 children and 116 adults, making a significant impact in the lives of these community members through our targeted aid.

Donation
December 22, 2022
Kolha Drive

In Kolha, our team carried out another successful drive, demonstrating our ongoing commitment to reaching out and supporting various communities in need.
